Number Synchronicity

Number Synchronicity or The Synchronicity of Numbers refers to meaningful coincidence when numbers appear in a certain pattern that can mean something to people.

Numbers have mystical meanings

Examples

Repeat Sequence Numbers

eg 1212, 1313, 1414

Sequential numbers

eg 123, 456, 789

Bookend Numbers

eg 1221, 1331, 1441

Mystery of Numbers

Repeat Sequence Numbers

You have found balance between your light and dark. You actively accept your role in co-creating with the universe to manifest your soul’s desires. Step forward in the direction of your true life purpose knowing that your material needs will be met as you take action.)

Sequential numbers

Indicate steps along your life path and where you are at any given point in your situation or journey.

Bookend Numbers

A situation in your life will be coming to a close soon. Utilise the lessons and move on to the next exciting part of your life.

Combination of synchronicity can be a powerful omen

Meaning of Numbers

0 Zero

Number 0 is the sign of fulfillment. It shows that you are on the right path. You don’t need to change anything. The energy of the Universe is with you and guiding you towards your ultimate goal of life.It is the sign of infinite possibilities and the free flow of ideas.

1 one

Representing the top order, the number is the sign of self-sufficiency. It shows your determination and brings out your capability as a self -reliant leader. The number one is also the indication that you have chosen negativity and are struggling to meet the ends. Number 1 is the great omen to start anything new and look for new beginnings.
